Hi! My name is Ashley Mazarin and I am currently a Junior attending Roslyn High School. It has been my mission to help out the Navajos on the Indian reservation in Arizona. Last year, I visited a few times and helped their community by chopping firewood, delivering groceries, and educating myself on their way of life. I ultimately decided I wanted to do more for them and thus, "Notebooks for Navajos" was created.


"Notebooks for Navajos" collected about 650 pounds of school supplies from my local elementary and middle school. I traveled down to Arizona with my dad and hand delivered the supplies to students and even some teachers. During these trying times I still am trying all I can to help their community
